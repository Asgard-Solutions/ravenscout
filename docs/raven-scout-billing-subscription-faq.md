# Raven Scout — Billing & Subscription FAQ

### How am I billed?
All Raven Scout purchases (Core, Pro, Extra Analytics packs) are billed by the platform you subscribed through — Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Raven Scout never charges your card directly.

### When do I get charged?
The first charge hits the day you subscribe; subsequent renewals are on the same calendar day every month or year. The Trial tier is free — no charge unless and until you subscribe.

### Where can I see my next renewal date?
Open **Profile → Subscription** in the app, or **Manage Subscriptions** in your App Store / Google Play account.

### How do I cancel?
- **iOS**: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Raven Scout → Cancel.
- **Android**: Play Store app → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Raven Scout → Cancel.
Access continues until the end of the paid-through period.

### How do I upgrade from Core to Pro?
**Profile → Subscription → Upgrade to Pro**. The platform prorates the difference; new Pro features unlock immediately.

### How do I downgrade from Pro to Core?
Same path — **Profile → Subscription → Switch to Core**. The change takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You keep Pro features until then.

### Do my saved hunts disappear if I downgrade?
No. Your local hunt history stays on your device. Cloud-backed (Pro-only) images remain accessible while your Pro period is still active; after Pro expires the app falls back to local copies on the device that originally created the hunt.

### Do saved hunts count against my monthly limit if I re-open them?
No. Only running a NEW AI analysis consumes a credit. Re-opening a saved hunt, editing overlays, repositioning markers, and offline viewing are all free.

### What about unused analyses at the end of the month?
- **Core**: unused analyses carry over for **1 billing cycle** (replace-mode — last month's unused balance carries into this month).
- **Pro**: unused analyses accumulate for up to **12 billing cycles**.
- Trial has no rollover — Trial credits are 3 lifetime, full stop.

### What if I run out mid-month?
Buy an **Extra Hunt Analytics** pack (+5 / +10 / +15) under **Profile → Subscription → Extra Analytics**. Packs are one-time, never expire, and drain after your monthly subscription credits are used up.

### What happens to my Trial when I use all 3 analyses?
You can still open every hunt you've already saved — the analysis output and overlays remain visible offline. To run a NEW analysis you'll need to subscribe to Core or Pro, or buy an Extra Analytics pack.

### I was charged but my Pro features aren't unlocked.
Force-close the app, reopen it, and pull-to-refresh on the Subscription page. If still stuck, email **support@asgardsolution.io** with your App Store / Play Store receipt number.

### I have a promo / redeem code — where do I enter it?
- iOS: App Store → profile → Redeem Gift Card or Code.
- Android: Play Store → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Redeem code.
Once redeemed, open Raven Scout and the tier upgrade flows through RevenueCat automatically.

### Can I get a refund?
See our [Refund Policy](raven-scout-refund-policy.md). The short version: Apple and Google handle refunds via their own policies; we'll always help you navigate them.
