# Raven Scout — Billing & Subscription FAQ

### How am I billed?
All Raven Scout subscriptions (Core, Pro) are billed by the platform you subscribed through — Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android). Raven Scout itself never charges your card directly.

### When do I get charged?
The first charge hits the day you subscribe; subsequent renewals are on the same calendar day monthly or yearly. The 14-day Trial is free — no charge unless you convert.

### Where can I see my next renewal date?
Open **Profile → Subscription** in the app, or view **Manage Subscriptions** in your App Store / Google Play account.

### How do I cancel?
- **iOS**: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Raven Scout → Cancel.
- **Android**: Play Store app → profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Raven Scout → Cancel.
Access continues until the end of the paid-through period.

### How do I upgrade from Core to Pro?
**Profile → Subscription → Upgrade to Pro**. The platform prorates the difference on your next renewal. New Pro features unlock immediately.

### How do I downgrade from Pro to Core?
Same path — **Profile → Subscription → Switch to Core**. The change takes effect at the end of the current billing period. You keep Pro features until then.

### Do my saved hunts disappear if I downgrade?
No. Your local hunt history stays on your device. Cloud-backed (Pro-only) images remain accessible for 30 days after the downgrade, then transition to local-only.

### Do my saved hunts count against my monthly limit if I re-open them?
No — only running a NEW AI analysis uses a credit. Re-opening a saved hunt, editing overlays, and offline viewing are all free.

### What happens to my Trial if I don't subscribe?
At day 15 the app locks you out of new analyses but keeps all previously saved hunts viewable. Subscribing any time re-enables new analyses.

### I was charged but my Pro features aren't unlocked.
Force-close the app, reopen it, and pull to refresh on the Subscription page. If still stuck, email **support@asgardsolution.io** with your App Store / Play Store receipt.

### I have a promo / redeem code — where do I enter it?
iOS: App Store → profile → Redeem Gift Card or Code.
Android: Play Store → profile → Payments & subscriptions → Redeem code.
Once redeemed, open Raven Scout and the tier upgrade flows through RevenueCat automatically.

### Can I get a refund?
See our [Refund Policy](raven-scout-refund-policy.md). The short version: Apple and Google handle refunds via their own policies; we'll always help you navigate them.
