# Raven Scout — Getting Started

Welcome. This guide walks you from install to your first AI hunt analysis in under five minutes.

## 1. Install the App
- **iOS**: search "Raven Scout" on the App Store.
- **Android**: search "Raven Scout" on Google Play.
Both builds are identical in features — your hunts sync to your account either way.

## 2. Create an Account
On the welcome screen tap **Create Account** and pick one:
- **Google** — one tap, uses your Google profile email.
- **Email + password** — a 6-digit code is emailed to verify.
A 14-day **Trial** unlocks automatically on signup. No card required.

## 3. Grant Permissions
The app will ask for:
- **Location** — used to pre-fill GPS / weather on every new hunt. You can deny and enter lat/lon manually.
- **Photo library** — to pick saved map screenshots.
- **Camera** — if you want to snap a map from another screen.
Everything except Location is fully optional.

## 4. Start Your First Hunt
From the home screen tap **NEW HUNT** → you'll walk through 5 wizard steps:
1. **Species** — pick deer, elk, turkey, hog, etc.
2. **Maps** — upload 1–4 map images OR use the built-in interactive map.
3. **Weapon & Method** — e.g. archery / still hunt.
4. **When & Where** — hunt date, time window, GPS, wind. Weather auto-fills.
5. **Confirm** — review, hit **ANALYZE HUNT**.

## 5. Read the Analysis
After ~15–45 seconds you'll land on the results screen:
- **Map with color-coded overlays** — green = stand, orange = corridor, blue = access route, red = avoid zone, etc. The legend at the bottom always matches the overlay colors.
- **Top Setups** — ranked 1–3, each with entry / exit / wind / thermals / pressure risk.
- **Summary, Wind Notes, Best Time, Key Assumptions, Species Tips** — scroll down.
- **Overlay editor** — tap any marker to rename, hide, or drag it.

## 6. Save, Revisit, Offline
- Every analysis auto-saves to **SAVED HUNTS** (back arrow → Saved Hunts).
- Tap any saved hunt to reopen the full analysis with overlays.
- Offline: saved hunts render with no signal — cellular dead zones are fine.

## 7. Upgrade When Ready
Trial → Core → Pro lives under **Profile → Subscription**. Purchases go through the App Store / Play Store via RevenueCat. Cancel any time in your store account.

## Troubleshooting Tips
- **Stuck on analyzing** — bad signal. Analysis needs a round-trip to our AI backend; try Wi-Fi.
- **"Limit reached"** — your tier hit its monthly hunt cap. Upgrade or wait for the next cycle.
- **Weather didn't fill in** — Location permission is denied, or you entered a future date past the forecast window. Enter manually.
