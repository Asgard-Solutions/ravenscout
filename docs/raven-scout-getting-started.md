# Raven Scout — Getting Started

Welcome. This guide walks you from install to your first AI hunt analysis in under five minutes.

## 1. Install the App
- **iOS**: search "Raven Scout" on the App Store.
- **Android**: search "Raven Scout" on Google Play.
Both builds are identical in features — your hunts sync to your account either way.

## 2. Create an Account
On the welcome screen tap **Create Account** and pick one:
- **Sign in with Apple** *(iOS / iPad only)* — one tap; uses your Apple ID. Supports Apple's **Hide My Email** relay so your real email never leaves your Apple account.
- **Continue with Google** *(Android only)* — one tap, uses your Google profile email.
- **Email + password** — available on both platforms; a 6-digit code is emailed to verify.
- **Biometric unlock** *(Face ID / Touch ID / Fingerprint)* — appears below if you've enrolled it on a previous sign-in.

Raven Scout shows the auth provider native to your platform (Apple on iOS, Google on Android) — there's never a mix-and-match login screen.

New accounts start on the **Trial** tier with 3 free AI hunt analyses (lifetime total — no time limit, no card required).

## 3. Grant Permissions
The app will ask for:
- **Location** — used to pre-fill GPS / weather on every new hunt. You can deny and enter lat/lon manually.
- **Photo library** — to pick saved map screenshots.
- **Camera** — if you want to snap a map from another screen.
Everything except Location is fully optional.

## 4. Start Your First Hunt
From the home screen tap **NEW HUNT** → you'll walk through the wizard:
1. **Species** — pick deer, elk, turkey, hog, etc.
2. **Maps** — upload up to 5 map images OR use the built-in interactive map. (On Trial / Core only the primary image is sent to the AI; Pro cross-references all of them.)
3. **Weapon & Method** — e.g. archery / still hunt.
4. **Conditions** — hunt date, time window, GPS, wind. Weather auto-fills for Core / Pro; Trial users fill manually.
5. **Confirm** — review, hit **ANALYZE HUNT**.

## 5. Read the Analysis
After ~15–45 seconds you'll land on the results screen:
- **Map with color-coded overlays** — green = stand, orange = corridor, blue = access route, red = avoid zone, etc. The legend at the bottom always matches the overlay colors.
- **Top Setups** — ranked 1–3, each with entry / exit / wind / thermals / pressure risk.
- **Summary, Wind Notes, Best Time, Key Assumptions, Species Tips** — scroll down.
- **Saved Markers panel** — long-press a marker to drag, tap to inspect, or use the Add Marker button to drop your own waypoints.

## 6. Save, Revisit, Offline
- Every analysis auto-saves to **SAVED HUNTS** (back arrow → Saved Hunts).
- Tap any saved hunt to reopen the full analysis with overlays.
- Offline: saved hunts render with no signal — cellular dead zones are fine.

## 7. Upgrade When Ready
Manage your tier under **Profile → Subscription**. Purchases go through the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) via RevenueCat. The paywall clearly shows the billed amount (e.g. "$79.99 /year"), auto-renewal terms, and functional links to the **Terms of Use (EULA)** and **Privacy Policy** — tap either to open them in your browser. Cancel any time in your store account.

## Troubleshooting Tips
- **Stuck on analyzing** — bad signal. Analysis needs a round-trip to our AI backend; try Wi-Fi.
- **"Limit reached"** — your tier hit its monthly hunt cap (or your Trial 3 are used up). Upgrade, buy an Extra Analytics pack, or wait for the next cycle.
- **Weather didn't fill in** — Trial tier doesn't include weather auto-fill, OR Location permission is denied, OR the date is past the forecast window. Enter wind manually.
